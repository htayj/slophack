# Creature Taxonomy

## Surface Creatures

### `@` — Humans
Fully sentient creatures with free will, driven by higher-order goals. They understand telos, have complex motivations, can talk, and balance multiple competing desires. Most unique per-instance compared to other creature types. They can play roles and have individual personalities.
- **Player** — the protagonist
- **Ranger** — NPC humans
- **Named NPCs** — unique individuals with specific roles

### `h` — Humanoids
Look and seem human but are not truly sentient. Driven only by animalistic desires (which can be complex). Capable of rudimentary thought and stereotyped vocalizations but lack true free will and object permanence. They perform player-like actions (eating, drinking, etc.) without understanding why. Manipulable through environmental control — what is in front of them is what is in their mind.
- **Hippie** — shuffled around by fate, no object permanence, susceptible to flags, can be "hippie trapped", always walk on roads, drink water only when told to, driven to alter their mind via substances out of pure curiosity. Herding cats. Can become goblins.
- **Wook** — festival goers with a primary desire to listen to music

### `K` — Kops
Enforcers of order. They police humanoids and are antagonists to humans. Maintain order among the humanoid population.
- **Acid Kop** — can only be seen while under the influence of psychedelics
- **Real Kop** — rare, only spawned in specific circumstances

### `E` — Egregores
Spiritual beings manifested from collective actions and events in the world. Born of mind made manifest. Invisible to humanoids (requires true sentience/a real mind to perceive). Not biological — formed from psychic/mental energy.
- **Lesser Egregore**
- **Greater Egregore**
- **Collective Egregore**

### `M` — Maws
Devourers. Driven by insatiable hunger for a specific target resource. They secretly steal from unsuspecting entities. Can shapeshift and appear as other entities.
- **Flag-eater** — devours flags
- **Food-eater** — devours food
- **Water-eater** — devours water/liquids
- **Time-eater** — devours time

### `V` — Vex
Flag creatures made of flags, with mystical flag powers. Generally neutral alignment.

### `f` — Felines
Cats and cat-like creatures. Subtypes TBD.

### `d` — Canids
Dogs and dog-like creatures. Subtypes TBD.

## Subterranean Creatures

### `c` — Clowns
Creepy demon-like creatures. Honk when they walk. Various subtypes TBD.

### `W` — Watchers
Stay in the shadows and out of sight. Like to watch and follow the player from a distance. Lurkers and stalkers.

### `w` — Worms
Spawn from dead things. Decomposers.

### `m` — Mushroom Creatures
Fungal beings.

### `a` — Acid Creatures
Advanced psionic/psychedelic beings. Similar in vibe to Protoss from StarCraft.

### `e` — Machine Elves
McKenna-style machine elves, not fantasy elves. Psychedelic entities.

### `g` — Goblins
Hoarders. Closely related to hippies — hippies can become goblins.
